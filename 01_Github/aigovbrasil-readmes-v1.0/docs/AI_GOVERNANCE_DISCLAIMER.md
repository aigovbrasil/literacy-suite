## Scope

- Provide medical, psychiatric, or psychological advice (`adhd-desk-dashboard`)

- Provide financial or investment advice
- Replace licensed professional services of any kind

## EU AI Act
EU AI Act Article 4 requires AI literacy as a legal obligation (in effect since February 2, 2025). This repository supports that literacy — it does not constitute legal compliance advice.

## Responsible use
Users are responsible for:
- Reviewing all AI-generated outputs before using them professionally
- Ensuring their use of these skills complies with applicable law
- Not relying on AI outputs for decisions requiring licensed professional judgment
